ABOUT Heliophysics Hackweek 2020

<

Heliophysics Hackweek 2020 is a five-day hackweek held virtually over a period of two weeks.
Participants will learn about technologies used to access and process
heliophysics data with a focus on machine learning.
The format will consist of project pitches, interactive lectures,
facilitated exploration of datasets, and hands-on software development.
