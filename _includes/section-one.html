ABOUT Heliophysics Hackweek 2020

Heliophysics Hackweek 2020 is a 5-day hackweek to be held at the University of Maryland. Participants will learn about technologies used to access and process heliophysics data with a focus on TBD. Mornings will consist of interactive lectures, and afternoon sessions will involve facilitated exploration of datasets and hands-on software development.
